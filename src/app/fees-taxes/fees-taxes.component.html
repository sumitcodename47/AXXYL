FEES AND TAXES

Airport Fees not included: all city airports with pickup and or drop off fees are subject to the fees of that airport. AXXYL inc does not pay these fees and taxes. All airport fees will be included into the fares calculated for those airports.

AXXYL will deduct the appropriate airport fees from each driver that it applies. the fares for all airports in which that driver is working at no extra cost to the drivers.

Billing statements will include number and amount billed of services to and from airports. These fees will be added to the original fares at the time of service request and acceptance.
