    Will AXXYL Have airport service

    Does AXXYL Have customer support contact info

    Will AXXYL have service for people with wheels chairs

Yes, there will be a service for disabled persons needing a ride with non electrical wheel chairs. Most drivers will be able to accept rides with one mechanical wheel chair depending on the vehicle that’s is chosen. Some may require an upgraded ride for availability and size of the vehicle. Note: some fees may apply.

    Will AXXYL have back round checks for the drivers

Yes, all driver are required to have a background check. If the driver is servicing the airport there will be special requirements. The airport will require additional requirements from drivers.

1: you must have a finger print background check and be approved. (Free of charge from the airport security Phoenix sky harbor)

2: you must be able to pick up any person with a disability and store a wheel chair in your vehicle along with 1 piece of luggage up to 26”x 19” Note: See airport requirements in terms and conditions.
