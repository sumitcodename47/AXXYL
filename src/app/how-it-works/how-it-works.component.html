How It Works

Axxyl is a mobile app designed for the Independent Contractors (drivers) in mind. For only $49.99 a month, you can schedule your own Flexible work hours and work weeks while also keeping 100% of the ride share fare earned. Axxyl, will use are team of experts to assess your feed back from drivers and customers to deliver outstanding service above all other ride sharing services.
